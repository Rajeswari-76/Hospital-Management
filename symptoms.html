Common Diseases & Symptoms

Here are some common diseases and their symptoms:

Disease Symptoms
Diabetes Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision
Hypertension Headache, dizziness, chest pain, shortness of breath
Asthma Wheezing, coughing, chest tightness, difficulty breathing
COVID-19 Fever, cough, loss of taste/smell, difficulty breathing
Migraine Severe headache, nausea, sensitivity to light/sound
